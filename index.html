Hello world! I'm a former Naval Officer turned software engineer, Charlotte, a.k.a. Chuck, Webster. I knew years prior to the end of my service that my future lay in software engineering, as it combined my loves of mathematics, language, and problem solving. My time in the Navy, surrounded in highly technical systems and machinery formed me into a technically curious individual, always wondering and exploring how and why things work the way they do.

It is with great excitement that I embark on this lifelong learning journey of a career in software engineering through attending The Rithm School. In my formative years, I double majored at the Unites States Naval Academy, earning a Bachelors of Science in Economics and International Relations, as well as rowing on the varsity crew team. I went on to serve eleven years as a Naval Officer and ship driver. During this time, I served as a technical subject matter expert in navigation and the ship's weapon's systems, leading a team of over 50 personnel in the maintenance and operation of all weapons systems onboard.
